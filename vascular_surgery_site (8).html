Vascular Surgery: A Student's Guide

About the Book

This textbook is written to help high school students understand the basics of vascular surgery. It covers everything from how veins and arteries work to what happens during surgery to treat vein-related diseases.

Why it's useful for students:

  • Explains anatomy and surgical topics in clear, simple language.
  • Helps prepare for biology, anatomy, or pre-med interest.
  • Inspires students to explore science and medicine early.
